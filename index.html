market-structure

  • Position trader => monthly-weekly-daily
  • Swing trader => daily-4h-1h
  • Short term trader => 4h-1h-1hm
  • Day & scalper => 1h-15m-5m
  • look for market structure shift in highest time frame of the three.
  • find support & resistance in middle time frame of the three and try to find equal s&r created by much higher time frame like daily or weekly.
  • entry should in the small time frame of the three and entry should be in any of the kill zone ( asia, london, ny ).
